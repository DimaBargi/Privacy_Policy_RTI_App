Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is this app a replacement for medical advice?
A: No, this app is designed to complement your medical treatment, not replace it. Always follow your healthcare team's instructions and consult them for any medical concerns.
Q: How often should I practice breathing exercises?
A: We recommend daily practice sessions of 5-10 minutes. Your radiation therapy team may provide specific guidance based on your treatment plan.
Q: Can I use this app during my actual treatment?
A: The app is primarily for practice and preparation. During actual treatment, follow your radiation therapy team's specific instructions for breathing techniques.
Q: What if I can't hold my breath for the recommended time?
A: The app allows you to customize timing settings. Start with shorter durations and gradually increase as you become more comfortable. Consult your medical team for personalized guidance.
Q: Is my practice data saved securely?
A: Yes, all your practice data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or transmit personal medical information to external servers.
Q: Can I export my treatment log?
A: Currently, treatment logs are stored within the app. We're working on export functionality for future updates. Contact support if you need assistance accessing your data.
